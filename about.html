About Transitional Justice for Gaza (TJG)

TJG is an initiative to address and cure the inefficiency and bias of the international order (see Context for more information), with the ultimate goal of delivering transitional justice to the victims and survivors of Gaza, who suffered from the Israeli attackes on and after 07 October 2023. TJG provides a channel for pro-Palestine/humanity individuals with the right expertise to contribute to these just cause and ends.
