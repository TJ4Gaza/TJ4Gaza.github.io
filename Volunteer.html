please send email to: transitionaljusticeforgaza@gmail.com, If you believe you have any of the following expertise:

  • Geospatial;
  • Digital forensics;
  • Criminal intelligence analysis;
  • Military operations;
  • Cybersecurity;
  • Graphic design; and
  • Database.

In the subject of your email, please indicate your name and your area of expertise. Please provide: a brief introduction about you, why would you want to volunteer, in what area you think you can contribute the most, best way to contact you (if not by email), and how did you hear about this initiative. TJG will get back to you the soonest possible to hear more about you, answer any questions you may have, and discuss how we can cooperate to advance the only declared goals of TJG.

Please be informed that:

  • TJG IS not affiliated to any country or other organizations;
  • TJG is not receiving any funding from any source; and
  • All efforts exerted as part of this initiative, including the founder's, will not be remunerated whatsoever.
